<p><p>The demineralized cancellous sponge market consists of two main types: strips and blocks. Strips are thin, flexible forms of bone graft material designed for easier application and integration into irregular bone sites, enhancing healing in minimally invasive procedures. Blocks, on the other hand, offer a rigid structure that provides stability for larger defects, making them ideal for reconstructive surgeries. Both types are used in various orthopedic and dental applications, capitalizing on their biocompatibility and osteoconductive properties.</p></p>
