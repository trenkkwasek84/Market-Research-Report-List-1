<p><p>The automotive expansion valve market consists of various types, including C Type thermostatic expansion valves (TXM), G Type thermostatic expansion valves (TXV), and V Type thermostatic expansion valves (TMX). C Type valves regulate refrigerant flow based on temperature, helping optimize performance in smaller systems. G Type valves offer enhanced control and efficiency, suitable for a range of vehicles. V Type valves, designed for variable conditions, enhance cooling efficiency in high-performance applications. Together, these valves play a crucial role in automotive air conditioning systems.</p></p>
