<p><strong>The Soy Dietary Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy dietary fibers market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I fibers are primarily soluble fibers, which dissolve in water and help in digestion, promote gut health, and regulate blood sugar. They are often used in functional foods and supplements. Type II fibers are insoluble and do not dissolve in water, contributing to bulk in the digestive system, aiding in regular bowel movements, and enhancing overall digestive health. Both types play significant roles in nutrition.</p></p>
