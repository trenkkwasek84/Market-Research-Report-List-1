<p><p>The Portable Nuclear Reactor market primarily includes two types: Thermal-neutron Reactors and Fast-neutron Reactors. Thermal-neutron Reactors utilize low-energy neutrons for fission, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including electricity generation and research. Fast-neutron Reactors, on the other hand, employ high-energy neutrons, enabling them to utilize a broader array of nuclear fuel and significantly improve fuel efficiency. Both types are gaining traction due to their potential for compact energy solutions and reduced carbon emissions.</p></p>
