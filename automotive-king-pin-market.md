<p><strong>The Automotive King Pin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressing Bushing Type</li><li>Floating Bushing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive king pin market is primarily divided into two types: pressing bushing type and floating bushing type. The pressing bushing type integrates a bushing pressed into the king pin housing, providing enhanced stability and durability, suited for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, the floating bushing type allows the bushing to move slightly within the housing, accommodating wear and improving alignment in dynamic conditions. Both types serve critical roles in steering and suspension systems, impacting vehicle handling and safety.</p></p>
