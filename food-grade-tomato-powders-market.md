<p><p>The Food Grade Tomato Powders market is primarily divided into two types: Spray Dried Type and Freeze Dried Type. Spray Dried Tomato Powder is produced by atomizing liquid tomato pulp into hot air, rapidly evaporating moisture and preserving flavor and nutrients. It offers a longer shelf life and is commonly used in soups and sauces. In contrast, Freeze Dried Tomato Powder involves freezing fresh tomatoes and removing moisture under vacuum, retaining more color, taste, and nutritional value, making it ideal for gourmet applications.</p></p>
