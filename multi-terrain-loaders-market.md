<p><p>The Multi Terrain Loaders market is categorized into two primary types: rubber-tracked and steel-tracked. Rubber-tracked loaders are preferred for their lighter weight and versatility on delicate surfaces, making them ideal for landscaping and construction in urban environments. In contrast, steel-tracked loaders offer enhanced traction and durability on rough terrains, such as construction sites with heavy materials. Each type caters to different operational needs and ground conditions, influencing factors like maneuverability, weight distribution, and surface impact.</p></p>
