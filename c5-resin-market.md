<p><p>The C5 resin market is primarily categorized into two types: flake C5 resin and powder C5 resin. Flake C5 resin is characterized by its solid, thin sheets, making it suitable for applications requiring easy blending and processing, often utilized in adhesives and coatings. In contrast, powder C5 resin is a fine, granular form that offers excellent flow properties and is ideal for applications like paints and sealants, where uniform distribution and quick dissolution are essential for performance.</p></p>
