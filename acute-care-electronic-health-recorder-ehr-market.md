<p><p>The Acute Care Electronic Health Record (EHR) market can be categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises EHR systems involve localized software installations within healthcare facilities, offering enhanced data control and security but requiring significant upfront investments and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based EHR solutions utilize remote servers to store patient data, facilitating easier access, scalability, and lower initial costs. This model allows healthcare providers to focus on patient care rather than IT infrastructure management.</p></p>
