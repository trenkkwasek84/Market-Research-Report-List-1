<p><ul><li>Vane Anemometer</li><li>Cup Anemometer</li><li>Thermal Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic anemometer market comprises various types, including vane, cup, and thermal anemometers. Vane anemometers use rotating blades to measure wind speed, making them suitable for HVAC applications. Cup anemometers feature three or four cups that catch the wind, commonly used in meteorological stations for accuracy. Thermal anemometers rely on the heat transfer principle, utilizing temperature changes to gauge airflow, ideal for low-speed air measurements. Each type serves specific needs in environmental monitoring and industrial applications.</p></p>
