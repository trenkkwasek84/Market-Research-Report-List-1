<p><p>The Adhesiveless Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) market is categorized into two main types: Single-sided Non-adhesive FCCL and Double-sided Non-adhesive FCCL. Single-sided non-adhesive FCCL features a layer of copper on one side, ideal for applications requiring minimal weight and flexibility. In contrast, double-sided non-adhesive FCCL has copper layers on both sides, offering enhanced conductivity and design flexibility for complex circuits. Both types are used in electronics, automotive, and telecommunications for their superior performance and durability.</p></p>
