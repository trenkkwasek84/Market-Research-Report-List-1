<p><p>The Medical Grayscale Display Market is categorized primarily into two types based on output signal depth: 10-bit and 11-bit. A 10-bit output signal can display 1,024 shades of gray, providing significant detail for medical imaging. An 11-bit output signal enhances this capability, allowing for 2,048 shades of gray, resulting in finer distinctions between subtle differences in tissue contrast. This increased grayscale resolution is particularly beneficial for radiology and diagnostic imaging, improving both diagnostic accuracy and patient care.</p></p>
