<p><strong>The Trailer Refrigeration Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vehicle Powered</li><li>Self-powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trailer refrigeration unit market is divided into two main types: vehicle-powered and self-powered systems. Vehicle-powered units utilize the truck's engine to operate the refrigeration system, offering a cost-effective solution for short hauls. In contrast, self-powered units have their own independent power source, such as diesel generators or batteries, enabling consistent temperature control regardless of the vehicle's operation. This makes self-powered units ideal for longer transports and diverse applications, ensuring flexibility and reliability in various transportation scenarios.</p></p>
