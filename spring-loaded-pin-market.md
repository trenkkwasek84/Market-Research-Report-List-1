<p><p>The Spring-Loaded Pin market consists of three primary types: Surface Mounted Type, Through Hole Type, and Floating Type. Surface Mounted Type pins are designed for easy installation directly onto circuit boards, offering stable connections. Through Hole Type pins pass through holes in the PCB, providing robust mechanical support. Floating Type pins allow for some movement, accommodating misalignments during assembly, thus enhancing connection reliability. Each type serves distinct applications in electronic devices, contributing to efficient performance and durability.</p></p>
