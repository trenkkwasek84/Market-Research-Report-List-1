<p><ul><li>Internal Drivers</li><li>External Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driver market is categorized into internal and external drivers. Internal drivers are components integrated within LED fixtures, providing power and control directly, offering compact designs and simplifying installations. Conversely, external drivers are standalone units that supply power to multiple LED fixtures, allowing for flexibility in installations and easier upgrades. Both types cater to different application needs, with internal drivers optimizing space and external drivers enhancing scalability and ease of maintenance in larger systems, contributing to overall market growth.</p></p>
