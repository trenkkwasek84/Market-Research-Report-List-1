<p><p>The electromagnetic braking systems market features various types, each serving unique applications. Single face brakes utilize a single friction surface for effective stopping power. Power off brakes rely on electrical disconnection to engage braking instantly. Particle brakes use magnetic particles for smooth, controllable braking action. Hysteresis power brakes generate torque through magnetic fields, allowing for precise control. Multiple disk brakes enhance friction surface area, improving efficiency and heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
