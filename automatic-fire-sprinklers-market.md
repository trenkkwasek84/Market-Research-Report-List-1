<p><strong>The Automatic Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Dry Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Preaction Fire Sprinklers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic fire sprinkler market encompasses various types catering to different needs. Wet fire sprinklers are filled with water and activate during heat exposure. Dry fire sprinklers contain pressurized air, releasing water only upon activation, ideal for cold environments. Deluge fire sprinklers release a large volume of water simultaneously across an area, suitable for high-risk places. Preaction fire sprinklers combine wet and dry systems, requiring detection before water release, making them effective in spaces needing protection from accidental discharge.</p></p>
