<p><ul><li>Powder Soil Amendment</li><li>Granular Soil Amendment</li><li>Liquid Soil Amendment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil amendment market is categorized into three main types: powder, granular, and liquid. Powder soil amendments are finely ground materials that enhance nutrient availability and soil structure, making them easy to incorporate. Granular soil amendments consist of larger particles, providing slow-release benefits and aiding in moisture retention. Liquid soil amendments are solutions applied directly to soil or plants, enabling quick nutrient absorption and improved soil health. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs, optimizing crop growth and soil quality.</p></p>
