<p><p>The supercapacitor activated carbon market focuses on two main applications: Electric Double-layer Capacitors (EDLCs) and Lithium-ion Capacitors (LICs). EDLCs utilize activated carbon electrodes to store energy through the electrostatic separation of charges, offering high power density and rapid charge/discharge cycles, ideal for applications like energy storage and regenerative braking. LICs combine the advantages of EDLCs and lithium-ion batteries, achieving higher energy density while maintaining fast charge capabilities, making them suitable for hybrid electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
