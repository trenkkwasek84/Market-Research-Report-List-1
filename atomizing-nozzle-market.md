<p><strong>The Atomizing Nozzle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Atomizing Nozzles</li><li>Hydraulic Atomizing Nozzles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomizing nozzle market is primarily categorized into pneumatic and hydraulic atomizing nozzles. Pneumatic atomizing nozzles use compressed air to break liquids into fine droplets, making them ideal for applications in coating, cooling, and spray drying. Hydraulic atomizing nozzles, on the other hand, utilize high-pressure liquid to achieve atomization, offering higher flow rates and suitable for processes like combustion and lubrication. Both types serve various industrial sectors, enhancing efficiency and performance in fluid handling applications.</p></p>
