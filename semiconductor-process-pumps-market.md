<p><p>The semiconductor process pumps market is categorized into two main types: liquid pumps and vacuum pumps. Liquid pumps are essential for transferring various chemicals and liquids used in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring precise flow and pressure control. Vacuum pumps, on the other hand, create low-pressure environments necessary for processes like chemical vapor deposition and etching. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of semiconductor fabrication processes, contributing to the overall reliability and performance of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
