<p><ul><li>Metalized Barrier Films</li><li>Transparent Barrier Films</li><li>White Barrier Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food packaging barrier film market consists of three main types: metalized barrier films, transparent barrier films, and white barrier films. Metalized barrier films are coated with a thin layer of metal to effectively prevent moisture and oxygen, enhancing shelf life. Transparent barrier films provide visibility while offering moderate protection against external elements. White barrier films primarily serve as a substrate that reflects light, ensuring better temperature control and protection against UV radiation, while maintaining product freshness.</p></p>
