Rotatable Transformation targets allow for continuous deposition by rotating during the sputtering process, leading to uniform material distribution and improved efficiency. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type targets are stationary and may be used for applications with less demand for uniformity or where cost is a concern. Each type serves distinct applications based on deposition requirements, material utilization, and production efficiency.</p></p>
