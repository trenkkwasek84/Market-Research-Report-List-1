<p><p>Laser distance sensors are devices that utilize laser technology to measure distances accurately and efficiently. They emit a laser beam that reflects off a target surface, allowing the sensor to calculate the distance based on the time it takes for the beam to return. These sensors are widely used across various industries including construction, manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare, owing to their precision, speed, and non-contact measurement capabilities.</p><p>The Laser Distance Sensors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.10% during the forecast period.
