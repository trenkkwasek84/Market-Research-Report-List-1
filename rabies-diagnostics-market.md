<p><p>The rabies diagnostics market encompasses several testing methodologies. The Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) detects rabies-specific antibodies in serum or cerebrospinal fluid, providing a reliable diagnosis. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) Assay is a molecular technique that amplifies rabies virus genetic material in samples, allowing for precise detection of the virus. Other methods may include rapid immunodiagnostic tests and viral isolation techniques. Together, these diagnostic tools enhance the ability to identify and manage rabies effectively in both humans and animals.</p></p>
