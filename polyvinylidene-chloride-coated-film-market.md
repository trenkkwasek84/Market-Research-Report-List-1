<p><p>The Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) coated film market is segmented into two main types: Coated PVDC One Side and Coated PVDC Two Side. Coated PVDC One Side features a coating applied to only one side of the film, enhancing barrier properties against moisture and gases while allowing the other side to retain flexibility and bonding capabilities. In contrast, Coated PVDC Two Side has coatings on both sides, providing superior protection and durability for applications requiring enhanced sealability and shelf life, particularly in food packaging.</p></p>
