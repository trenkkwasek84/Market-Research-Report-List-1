<p><p>The Schottky rectifier market is categorized into single and dual types. Single Schottky rectifiers consist of a single diode, offering high efficiency and low forward voltage drop, making them ideal for applications like power supplies. Dual Schottky rectifiers, on the other hand, integrate two diodes in a single package, allowing for space savings and simplified circuit design. Both types are essential in various sectors, including automotive and consumer electronics, due to their fast switching capabilities and thermal performance.</p></p>
