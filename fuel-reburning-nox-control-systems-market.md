<p><strong>The Fuel Reburning NOx Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) Reaction</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Reaction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel reburning NOx control systems are essential for reducing nitrogen oxide emissions from combustion processes. The Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) reaction utilizes ammonia or urea injected into flue gas at high temperatures, promoting NOx conversion without catalysts. In contrast, Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) reaction employs a catalyst to enhance the reaction efficiency at lower temperatures, improving NOx reduction rates. Both technologies are vital for industries aiming to comply with stringent environmental regulations while promoting cleaner energy production.</p></p>
