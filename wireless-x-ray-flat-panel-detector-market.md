<p><strong>The Wireless X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless X-ray flat panel detector market is primarily categorized into two types: direct conversion and indirect conversion. Direct conversion detectors use a semiconductor material, typically amorphous selenium, to directly convert X-rays into electrical signals, offering high image quality and efficiency. Indirect conversion detectors utilize a two-step process involving a scintillator to convert X-rays into visible light, which is then captured by a photodiode to generate an image. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for various applications in medical imaging.</p></p>
