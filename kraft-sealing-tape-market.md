<p><p>The Kraft Sealing Tape market includes two main types: Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape and Water Free Kraft Paper Tape. Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape requires moisture to bond effectively, making it ideal for secure packaging and environmentally friendly options as it is biodegradable. In contrast, Water Free Kraft Paper Tape uses adhesives that do not rely on water for adhesion, offering instant stickiness and convenience. Both types cater to different sealing needs in shipping and packaging applications.</p></p>
