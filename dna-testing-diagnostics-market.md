<p><p>The DNA Testing & Diagnostics market encompasses various methodologies for disease detection and genetic analysis. PCR-based diagnostics utilize polymerase chain reaction to amplify specific DNA sequences, enabling rapid detection of pathogens and genetic conditions. ISH (In Situ Hybridization) diagnostics allow for the localization of specific DNA or RNA sequences in tissues, aiding in cancer diagnostics and research. Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) offers comprehensive genomic profiling, allowing for detailed insights into genetic variations and mutations, enhancing personalized medicine approaches.</p></p>
