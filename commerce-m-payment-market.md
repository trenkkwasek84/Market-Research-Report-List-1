Peer-to-Peer Transfer allows users to send money directly to each other via mobile apps, facilitating easy transactions. NFC enables contactless payments through smartphones or wearables, providing convenience at retail locations. Barcode payments involve scanning codes at checkout, linking customers to their payment methods. Each type enhances transaction speed and customer experience in the evolving mobile commerce landscape.</p></p>
