<p><p>The Anti-sniper Detection System market is categorized into three main types: infrared, laser, and acoustic systems. **Infrared systems** utilize thermal imaging to detect heat signatures from snipers, providing real-time tracking capabilities. **Laser systems** employ laser-based technologies to identify sniper positions through reflected light, offering precision targeting. **Acoustic systems** analyze sound waves generated by gunfire to pinpoint shooter locations. Each type enhances situational awareness and ensures effective responses to potential sniper threats in military and security environments.</p></p>
