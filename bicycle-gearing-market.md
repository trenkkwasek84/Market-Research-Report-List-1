<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bicycle Gearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External(Derailleur)</li><li>Internal(Hub)</li><li>Internal(Bottom Bracket)</li><li>Internal and External Combined</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bicycle gearing systems can be categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. External (derailleur) systems use an external mechanism to shift gears on the bike's cassette. Internal (hub) gears are integrated within the wheel hub, offering protection and lower maintenance. Internal (bottom bracket) systems are housed within the bottom bracket for compact design. Some bikes combine internal and external gearing for versatility. Additionally, "others" includes specialized systems or hybrids, catering to diverse cycling needs and preferences.</p></p>
