<p><ul><li>Frontal Air Intake</li><li>Lateral Air Intake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber air intake market is segmented into two primary types: frontal air intake and lateral air intake. Frontal air intakes are designed to channel air directly from the front of the vehicle, optimizing airflow for increased performance and efficiency. In contrast, lateral air intakes draw air from the sides, benefiting from reduced aerodynamic drag while enhancing engine performance. Both types leverage carbon fiber's lightweight and durable properties, making them popular choices for automotive enthusiasts seeking improved performance and aesthetics.</p></p>
