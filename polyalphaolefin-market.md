<p><p>The polyalphaolefin (PAO) market is segmented into three main types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity PAOs are typically used in applications requiring excellent fluidity and low-temperature performance, such as in engine oils. Medium viscosity PAOs offer balanced properties suitable for a wide range of industrial lubricants and automotive applications. High viscosity PAOs provide superior thermal stability and are ideal for heavy-duty applications, such as in gear oils and hydraulic fluids, offering enhanced performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>
