<p><p>The fireproof cable market encompasses various types designed to enhance safety in electrical installations. Flame retardant cables limit the spread of fire by self-extinguishing. Low Smoke Zero Halogen (LSOH) cables produce minimal smoke and toxic emissions when exposed to fire, making them safer for indoor environments. Low Smoke Flexible (LSF) cables offer similar benefits but with added flexibility for easier installation. Fire-resistant cables maintain circuit integrity under high temperatures, ensuring critical systems remain operational during a fire, thereby enhancing overall safety.</p></p>
