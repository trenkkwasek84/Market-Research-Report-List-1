<p><ul><li>Portable Radiation Monitors</li><li>Transportable Dosimetric Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation monitors market includes various types designed for specific applications. Portable radiation monitors are lightweight, handheld devices ideal for personal safety and immediate radiation detection, often used in emergency situations or fieldwork. In contrast, transportable dosimetric monitors are larger, mobile units that provide continuous measurement of radiation exposure over time, suitable for monitoring environments like nuclear facilities or research labs. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety and compliance in environments where radiation is present.</p></p>
