<p><p>Immersible ultrasonic transducers are categorized into three market types based on their vibration orientation: bottom vibration, side vibration, and top vibration. Bottom vibration transducers emit sound waves from beneath the surface, ideal for applications requiring deep penetration. Side vibration transducers project waves from the sides, providing effective cleaning and processing in confined spaces. Top vibration transducers generate ultrasonic waves from above, suitable for surface-level applications. Each type is tailored for specific industrial and commercial needs, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
