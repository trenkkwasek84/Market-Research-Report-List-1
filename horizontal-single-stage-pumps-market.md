<p><p>Horizontal single-stage pumps are widely used in various applications due to their efficient design. The single-suction type draws fluid from a single inlet, making it suitable for handling fluids with lower flow rates and less turbulence. In contrast, the double-suction type features two inlets, allowing for greater flow rates and reduced axial thrust, which enhances durability and stability. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with the single-suction type favoring simplicity, while the double-suction type offers higher efficiency in demanding scenarios.</p></p>
